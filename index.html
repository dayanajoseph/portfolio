about me

Hello, I am a highly motivated and dedicated individual with a strong background in project management and software engineering. With a degree in Project Management and a Master's in Computer Applications (MCA), I possess a solid foundation in both technical and managerial aspects of the IT industry. I firmly believe that IT and business are interconnected, and I strive to bridge the gap between these two domains.
